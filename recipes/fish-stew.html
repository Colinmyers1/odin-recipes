Hearty Fish Stew

A robust and flavorful fish stew, combining chunks of salmon and cod with mussels in a rich broth. Enhanced with fresh herbs, this stew is served in a large wooden bowl, accompanied by slices of dark rye bread, making for a hearty and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb salmon, cut into chunks
  • 1 lb cod, cut into chunks
  • 1 lb mussels, cleaned
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups fish stock
  • 2 cups white wine
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 leek, sliced
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme
  • 1 tsp fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 loaf dark rye bread

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and saut√© until soft.
  2. Add the carrots, celery, and leek, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in the fish stock and white wine. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the salmon, cod, and mussels to the pot. Cook until the fish is opaque and the mussels have opened, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Stir in the thyme and dill, and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve the stew hot with slices of dark rye bread on the side.
