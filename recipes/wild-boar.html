Roasted Wild Boar with Herbs and Root Vegetables

This dish features a succulent wild boar roast, seasoned with a blend of garlic, rosemary, thyme, and olive oil. Roasted to perfection, it is served with a medley of root vegetables, including carrots, parsnips, and potatoes, all infused with the rich flavors of mead.

Ingredients

  • 1 wild boar roast (about 5 lbs)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large carrots, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 4 parsnips, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 4 potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 cup mead

  3. Place the boar in a roasting pan. Surround it with the carrots, parsnips, and potatoes.
  4. Pour the mead over the vegetables.
  5. Roast in the oven for about 2.5 to 3 hours, or until the meat reaches an internal temperature of 160째F (71째C) and the vegetables are tender.
  6. Let the roast rest for 15 minutes before carving and serving.
