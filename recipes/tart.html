Berry and Nut Tart

This delightful dessert consists of a golden crust filled with a mixture of fresh berries and chopped nuts, all drizzled with honey. The tart is baked to a perfect crisp and served on a wooden platter, offering a sweet and rustic end to a Viking feast.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 2 cups mixed berries (blueberries, raspberries, blackberries)
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (hazelnuts, almonds, walnuts)
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out the pie crust and fit it into a tart pan. Prick the bottom with a fork.
  3. In a bowl, mix the berries, nuts, honey, lemon juice, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the berry mixture into the crust, spreading it out evenly.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the filling is bubbling.
  6. Let the tart cool before serving. Drizzle with extra honey if desired.
